AutoHotkey Cookbook

A Howto cut-n-past guide for AutoHotkey

Some example scripts are so huge that it would be poor coding practice to simple cut-n-paste them into your main script. For those AutoHotkey, (as usual) has the answer: 
#Include C:\Users\alexx\Documents\ahk\some_huge_script_by_someone_else.ahk
Here we will include some and link to others.
