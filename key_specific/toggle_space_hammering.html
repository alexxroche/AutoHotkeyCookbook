Shift-Space toggles on spacebar hammering until space is pressed to toggle it off 
+$Space::
    ; +Space (Shift-Space) toggles on spacebar hammering until 
    ; space is pressed to toggle it off
    KeyWait, Space
    While not GetKeyState("Space","P") loop {
        Send, {Space}
    }
return
